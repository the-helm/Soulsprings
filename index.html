FAQ

Questions? We're Here.

Do I need to book in advance? +
Yes, we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to secure your preferred time and therapist.
How long are treatments? +
Treatments range from 45 minutes to 3 hours, depending on the service. Our team will confirm duration at booking.
Can couples book together? +
Absolutely! We offer couples massage and side-by-side treatments in our luxury suites.
What products do you use? +
We use premium, dermatologically-tested skincare and wellness products from leading global brands.
Are gift vouchers available? +
Yes, we offer luxury gift vouchers for all occasions. Contact us or visit our spa to purchase.
